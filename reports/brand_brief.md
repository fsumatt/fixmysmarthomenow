# Fix My Smart Home Now - Brand Brief

## Brand role

This brand should feel:
- practical
- reliable
- technical but approachable
- calm under failure
- anti-hype

The vibe is not luxury smart home and not playful gadget clutter.
It should feel like the place you go when your automations are flaky and you want the shortest path to a stable setup.

## Positioning

- Reliability over novelty
- Interoperability over brand tribalism
- Diagnosis before buying
- Calm operator energy

## Logo direction

Need a simple logo mark that can scale to:
- favicon
- header/footer mark
- social preview use
- small product/site branding

### Good motifs
- house + signal/check
- house + node graph
- switch/plug/sensor icon with a stability cue
- simple hub/network motif
- subtle shield/check element

### Avoid
- cartoon smart home art
- overly cute robot mascot
- futuristic neon tech bro look
- anything too close to NDZ branding

## Color direction

Reuse NDZ structure, but shift the accent palette.
Suggested direction:
- deep slate / charcoal base
- electric teal or blue-green accent
- lighter cyan/teal secondary accents

That keeps the trust/reliability feel while separating it from NDZ.

## Standardization rule

Use the same overall house design system as NDZ for future sites:
- same layout system
- same content shell
- same build conventions
- new logo + brand colors per site
