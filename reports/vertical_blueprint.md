# Fix My Smart Home Now - Vertical Blueprint

## Positioning

**Tagline:** Smart home reliability without the guesswork.

This site should win by being the place people go when their smart home setup is flaky, fragmented, or confusing.

## Why this vertical is attractive

- high search demand for troubleshooting and setup failures
- messy fragmented SERPs
- weak current competition (generic blogs, Reddit, scattered videos)
- natural commerce fit across hubs, sensors, switches, plugs, bridges, repeaters, and controllers

## Primary user promises

1. Figure out why the setup is failing
2. Pick the right protocol/ecosystem
3. Stabilize the network or hub layer
4. Buy the smallest useful fix, not random gear

## Proposed architecture

### Entry / routing
- `/`
- `/start/`

### Main hubs
- `/protocols/` - Zigbee vs Z-Wave vs Thread vs Matter vs Wi-Fi
- `/troubleshooting/` - pairing failures, disconnects, latency, ghost/offline states
- `/hubs/` - best hub for mixed smart home, hub strategy, bridge strategy
- `/wifi-load/` - router/device-count/reliability under load
- `/devices/` - plugs, switches, bulbs, sensors, locks, cameras, bridges
- `/products/` - curated reliability-first picks

### Supporting page families
- protocol comparisons
- device-type troubleshooting
- ecosystem interoperability pages
- 'why won't X connect' pages
- 'best hub for mixed smart home' pages
- Wi-Fi capacity / segmentation / IoT SSID pages
- room/use-case pages if needed later

## Example early keyword clusters

### Reliability troubleshooting
- why won't my smart plug connect to wifi
- smart lights keep disconnecting
- smart home devices keep going offline
- why won't my smart bulb pair
- alexa device unresponsive but wifi works
- google home device offline fix

### Protocol / architecture decisions
- zigbee vs zwave which is better
- matter vs zigbee
- thread vs zigbee
- best hub for mixed smart home
- do i need a hub for smart lights

### Wi-Fi reliability / capacity
- how many devices can wifi handle smart home
- too many smart devices on wifi
- smart home separate ssid
- 2.4ghz smart home setup best practices

## Content model

### Hubs
Own the category and route deeper.

### Decision pages
Help users choose protocol, hub strategy, or network layout.

### Troubleshooting pages
Symptom -> likely cause -> exact fix.

### Commercial pages
Curated, scenario-based, reliability-first.

## Monetization model

- curated product pages
- comparison pages with buyer routing
- accessory/system bundles by scenario
- protocol/hub/device recommendations where context is strong

## Rules

- reliability first, novelty second
- fewer, better recommendations
- treat ecosystem fit as part of the product recommendation
- prefer structured decision trees over listicles
